Mohan Babu University (MBU)is a private multidisciplinary university located
in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India. The institution was officially established in
2022after receiving university status from the Government of Andhra Pradesh. It
evolved from the well-known educational institutions founded by Indian actor,
educationist, and philanthropist Mohan Babu. His vision was to create a modern
educational ecosystem that combines quality academics, professional skills, and
ethical values for students across India.
The roots of the university date back to 1992, when the Sri Vidyanikethan
Educational Trustwas founded to promote higher education in the Rayalaseema
region. Initially, the trust started with a small number of programs and a limited
student population. Over the next three decades, the institution expanded
rapidly and established multiple colleges and schools under the Sri
Vidyanikethan group. One of the major institutions developed during this period
was Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College, which became a prominent
technical institute in the region.
The university campus is spread across approximately 40–50 acresof land in
Tirupati and contains several academic blocks, research laboratories, libraries,
hostels, and sports facilities. Today, the university serves more than 10,000
studentsenrolled in various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral
programs. The institution employs around 600+ faculty membersand academic
staff who support teaching, research, and innovation activities across
departments.
Mohan Babu University currently offers more than 50 academic programsacross
disciplines such as engineering, computer science, data science, management,
commerce, and humanities. The engineering and technology programs are
among the most popular, with specializations including Artificial Intelligence,
Machine Learning, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Electronics. Each year, the
university admits thousands of students through national and state-level
entrance examinations as well as its own admission processes.
The university also places strong emphasis on research and innovation. It has
established multiple research centers and innovation labs that encourage
students to work on real-world problems in areas such as smart technologies,
digital transformation, and advanced computing. In addition, students are
encouraged to participate in hackathons, workshops, and industry collaborations
to improve their practical skills.
Apart from academics, the university promotes holistic development through
extracurricular activities. Students can participate in 20+ clubs and student
organizations, including cultural clubs, technical clubs, entrepreneurship cells,
and sports teams. Annual events, cultural festivals, and technical symposiums
attract participation from thousands of students across the region.
Over the years, Mohan Babu University has continued to expand its
infrastructure, academic offerings, and research initiatives. By combining 30+
years of educational legacy, modern technology-driven programs, and strong
industry connections, the university aims to prepare students to succeed in both
national and global professional environments.
