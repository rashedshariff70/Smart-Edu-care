How SmartEduCare Works

A seamless, fully automated flow — from university onboarding to real-time student AI support, college rules chatbot, and family health monitoring.

STEP 01
University Registration

Universities register through the secure portal. Credentials are verified and forwarded to the SmartEduCare admin for review.

Secure Submission
STEP 02
Admin Review & Email Response

Admin approves or rejects the application — sending login credentials on approval, or a clear reason with a reapply option on rejection.

Approve → Credentials Reject → Reason
STEP 03
Student Data Upload

Approved universities download the standard reference template, fill in all required student details, and upload the completed file securely.

Standard Template
STEP 04
Auto Credential Generation

Unique login credentials are automatically generated for each student and emailed to the university for distribution. Zero manual effort.

Fully Automated
STEP 05
Student AI Dashboard & Chatbot

Students log in to view their academic performance and chat with a personalised AI advisor — powered by LLaMA & GPT-OSS via Groq — via text or voice (Whisper STT).

Text & Voice AI
STEP 06
College Rules Chatbot — LLaMA 4 Scout

Students and staff ask questions about college regulations, fee structures, hostel policies, and academic calendars. LLaMA 4 Scout 17B reads uploaded PDF/DOCX rule documents and responds with precise, document-grounded answers.

PDF/DOCX Grounded
STEP 07
SmartPass — Parent AI Portal

Parents access SmartPass to chat with Gemini 2.5 Flash, get polite structured answers about their child, trigger deep AI analysis via Qwen, and receive a full PDF report by email.

AI PDF Report via Email

AI & LLM

Multi-LLM Architecture,
Built for Real Use

Six production LLMs — each chosen for its specific strength. No generic AI wrappers. Real models, real context, real answers.

Student Chat — LLaMA & GPT-OSS 120B

Two switchable LLMs via Groq power student Q&A — loaded with each student's full academic context for personalised, instant responses about grades, attendance, and college rules.

Parent Chat — Gemini 2.5 Flash

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash handles all parent interactions — polite, structured, multilingual responses plus TTS voice output powered by Gemini TTS with Groq TTS as fallback.

Voice Input — OpenAI Whisper STT

Whisper transcribes audio to text for both student and parent portals. Speak your question — the system processes it through the right LLM and speaks the answer back via TTS.

Deep Analysis — Qwen 3-32B

Qwen 2.5-32B-Instruct performs deep student analysis — outputting strict JSON with strengths, weaknesses, risk level, and improvement plan — which is auto-converted to a PDF report and emailed.

College Rules Chatbot — LLaMA 4 Scout 17B

Meta's LLaMA 4 Scout 17B (16E instruct) reads admin-uploaded PDF and DOCX college rule documents via Groq and answers precise questions about regulations, hostel policies, fee structures, and academic calendars — grounded strictly in the uploaded documents.

Context Builder — Excel + PDF/DOCX Parser

At login, the system reads student Excel data, extracts college rules from PDFs/DOCXs, and assembles a structured context file fed directly into the active LLM — zero manual prompt engineering.
