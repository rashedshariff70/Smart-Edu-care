Student Query System

Why is the Query System Important?

The query system allows students to communicate their concerns, questions, or suggestions directly to the administration. This ensures that their issues are addressed in a timely and organized manner.

It improves transparency, fosters trust, and provides an official channel to resolve academic, administrative, or personal issues effectively.

Regulations and Rules for Submitting Queries

  • Please ensure that your query is relevant to the academic, administrative, or extracurricular aspects governed by the college, and avoid unrelated personal or external matters
  • Please submit your query only if it is essential and directly related to college matters.
  • Use respectful and professional language.
  • Submit one query at a time to avoid confusion.
  • Avoid slang or informal language.
